PREP TECHNIQUES Coffee

At Coffo, we believe that every exceptional cup of coffee begins long before the brewing process. The steps we take before making your coffee—our “pre-technique”—are just as important as the brewing method itself. Here’s how we ensure that every cup you enjoy is the best it can be: Consistent Grinding: Just before brewing, we grind our beans to the ideal coarseness for each method, ensuring maximum freshness and taste. Proper Storage: Beans are stored in airtight containers, away from light and moisture, to preserve their flavor. Select Quality Beans: Everything starts with fresh, high-quality coffee beans. We source ours from trusted growers . Pure Water: We always use filtered water, because great coffee can only come from great water. Measure Precisely: The right ratio of coffee to water is crucial—so we weigh both for consistency in every cup. By focusing on these foundational steps, we set the stage for an unforgettable coffee experience made with love—from the very beginning.
